Torque control from ROS:
Start the ros node 
ros2 launch ceeorobot_control start_robot.launch.py 

Switch to the torque controller:
roscontrol set_controller_state scaled_joint_trajectory_controller inactive 
roscontrol set_controller_state forward_effort_controller active 

Set the teachpendant to Local control and activate the remote control *program*

Now you can send torque commands:
rostopic pub /forward_effort_controller/commands std_msgs/msg/Float64MultiArray "{data:[0.0,-2.0,0.0,0.0,0.0,0.0]}"
